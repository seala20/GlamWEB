glamconnect?

GlamConnect is a South African C2C e-commerce platform that connects sellers and buyers of clothes, makeup, and home designs.everyone can list their items and start selling for free ;or shop for second hands finds safely and conveniently.

To sell an item on GlamConnect:

Simply create an account, go to your dashboard, and click on "List a Product". Fill in the product details and upload photos. Once the listing is complete, your product will be available for buyers to browse and purchase.

Yes, GlamConnect is free to use.

You can create an account, list products, and browse the platform without any charges.This means once the order is complete, you will receive 100% of the asking price to your Glamconnect Wallet.

To contact GlamConnect support:

You can contact our support team via the "Contact Us" page on our website or email us directly at support@glamconnect.co.za.
