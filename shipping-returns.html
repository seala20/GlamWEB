Shipping & Returns

We partner with trusted delivery companies to make sure your GlamConnect orders get to you safely and quickly.

PAXI

PAXI offers affordable, convenient delivery to participating retail locations nationwide. With PAXI, you can choose to collect your order at a point closest to you.
Estimated delivery: 5–7 business days.

Aramex

Aramex provides reliable door-to-door delivery services across South Africa and internationally. Perfect for quick and secure delivery directly to your home or office.
Estimated delivery: 1–3 business days.

PostNet

PostNet offers counter-to-counter shipping across their network of locations. You can collect your package at the PostNet closest to you.
Estimated delivery: 2–4 business days.

Returns Policy

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 14 days of receiving your order. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in resellable condition.

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was defective or incorrectly delivered. To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number.
